Ctrl Alt Compete

The African Bank’s Ctrl Alt Compete challenges began in July 2019 among smaller teams in IT. This year, the Omni Development team has decided to spread the challenge and invite all African Bankers to take part in the fun!

What's a Hackathon??

A hackathon is an event, usually hosted by a tech company or organisation (in our case a department), where programmers and other people get together for a short period of time to collaborate on a project or tasks usually around problem solving by means of computer programming. With Ctrl Alt Compete, you don’t need to be a computer programmer! J This competition is for everyone.
